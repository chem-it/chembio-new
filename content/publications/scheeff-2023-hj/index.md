abstract: Nucleoside analogues are effective antiviral agents, and the continuous
  emergence of pathogenic viruses demands the development of novel and structurally
  diverse analogues. Here, we present the design and synthesis of novel nucleoside
  analogues with a carbobicyclic core, which mimics the conformation of natural ribonucleosides.
  Employing a divergent synthetic route featuring an intermolecular Diels-Alder reaction,
  we successfully synthesized carbobicyclic nucleoside analogues with high antiviral
  efficacy against respiratory syncytial virus.
