abstract: AbstractWe report a convenient and diastereoselective copper‐catalyzed reductive alkynone aldol addition to aldehydes, with pinacolborane as stoichiometric reductant, that results in the generation of densely functionalized, trisubstituted (Z)‐enone products analogous to Morita‐Baylis‐Hillman adducts. We demonstrate that the reaction proceeds via an allenolborate, which we have characterized by NMR spectroscopy. A range of aromatic and aliphatic aldehydes can be reductively coupled with various non‐terminal alkynones.
